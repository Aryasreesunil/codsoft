Online Job Portal

❮ ❯

The Online Job Portal is a dynamic web-based application designed to simplify and automate the job recruitment process. It acts as an interactive platform connecting job seekers and employers, allowing users to register, manage profiles, search or post job openings, and streamline the entire hiring workflow. The system features three key modules: Job Seeker, Employer, and Admin, each tailored to handle distinct functionalities like job applications, candidate shortlisting, and administrative approvals.
